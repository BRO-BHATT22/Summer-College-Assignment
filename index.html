University of Michigan

General Info

The University of Michigan, established in 1817 in Ann Arbor, is a leading public research university. It offers a wide range of top-ranked programs, particularly in engineering, business, law, and medicine. Known for its vibrant campus life, diverse student body, and strong research focus, U-M fosters innovation and public service. Its alumni network is influential worldwide, and the university consistently ranks among the best in the U.S
